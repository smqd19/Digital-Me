PROJECT: BOOKIFY - AI Hotel Voice Assistant
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Category: Voice AI | Conversational AI | Hospitality Tech

OVERVIEW:
Bookify is an enterprise-grade AI voice assistant designed for the hospitality 
industry. It handles real-time room bookings, availability checks, reservation 
modifications, and guest inquiries through natural voice conversations—replacing 
traditional IVR systems and scripted call flows.

KEY FEATURES:
• Natural Language Understanding: Processes complex booking requests, date 
  changes, room preferences, and special accommodations through free-form speech
• Real-Time System Integration: Connects directly to Property Management Systems 
  (PMS), booking engines, and inventory databases to execute actions during calls
• Sub-300ms Latency: Optimized voice pipeline ensuring natural, responsive 
  conversations without awkward pauses
• Multi-Turn Dialogue: Maintains context across conversation turns for complex 
  booking scenarios (e.g., "Actually, make that two rooms instead")
• Confirmation & Validation: Reads back booking details and confirms with 
  guests before executing changes

TECHNICAL ARCHITECTURE:
• Speech-to-Text: Real-time ASR with streaming transcription
• LLM Reasoning: Intent classification, slot filling, and dialogue management
• Tool Calling: Function calls to PMS APIs for availability, pricing, and booking
• Text-to-Speech: Natural voice synthesis with hospitality-appropriate tone
• State Management: Session-based context tracking for multi-turn conversations

TARGET INDUSTRIES:
• Hotels & Resorts
• Hospitality Chains
• Vacation Rentals
• Online Travel Agencies (OTAs)
• Booking Platforms

IMPACT:
• Eliminated rigid IVR menus and "press 1 for..." experiences
• Enabled 24/7 booking capability without staffing constraints
• Reduced average call handling time through efficient voice interactions
• Improved guest satisfaction with natural, conversational booking experience

Tech Stack: Python, FastAPI, OpenAI GPT, Speech-to-Text, Text-to-Speech, 
WebSockets, Real-time Streaming, REST APIs
